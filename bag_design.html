Purpose and Summary

This website is designed to showcase and sell the products of our floral shop. Visitors can browse a variety of floral arrangements and make purchases online. The site also provides information about the shop's services, hours of operation, and contact information.

Intended Audience

The intended audience for this website includes people who are interested in purchasing flowers for a variety of occasions, such as weddings, funerals, and holidays. The site is also targeted towards those who appreciate the artistry and beauty of floral arrangements.

Message and Purpose

The message of this website is to provide customers with a convenient and easy way to purchase high-quality floral arrangements. The site exists to promote the floral shop's products and services, increase customer satisfaction, and generate revenue for the business.

Scope

This website will cover the products and services offered by the floral shop, as well as information about the shop's history, staff, and location. The site will not cover other types of merchandise or services that are not related to the floral shop.

Goals

  1. To increase online sales and revenue for the floral shop
  2. To provide a user-friendly and visually appealing interface for customers
  3. To increase customer satisfaction and loyalty
  4. To promote our shop's products and services

Navigational Tools

The site will provide a navigation bar that includes links to the following pages: Home, Products, Contact, Our Story, Order Form, Links and additional information. The Home page will include introductory pharagraph and featured products. The Products page will include a gallery of products and their description and prices. The Info page will provide additional information about the shop's inventory status about the products. The Our Story page will include information about the shop's establishment and background stories. The Contact Us page will include a contact information including the shop's phone number and address. Finally, The Form page contains payment forms for customers' orders.
